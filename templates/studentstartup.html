ABOUT

This programme is created to foster and promote the spirit of entrepreneurship in students and faculties, SP-TBI provides the facility of Student Startup Programme where students from Bhavans Campus and our collaborated colleges with SP-TBI with startup or business ideas can take the help of SP-TBI, its resources, and services to become a full-fledged startup.

This programme will give provide the student startups with discounted incubation.

Facilities Provided

Plug-and-Play Dedicated Office

Access to Tech Lab

Access to our top-class mentors

Access to our events & webinars

Access to our talent pool

Access to seed funding up to INR 25 Lacs

Access to prototype grant up to INR 10 Lacs

AWS Credits worth 10000 USD

All ad-hoc services including CA, Registration, IP, etc

MathWorks Accelerator Program

Who is Eligible to Apply

  • One of the co-founders should be a Student/Faculty respectively. Please provide valid proof of the same.
  • Startups at Ideation, Prototype, Traction & Scaling stages.
  • Startups from the science & technology sector.
  • Entrepreneurs and ideators with registered startups less than 7 years old from the date of registration.
